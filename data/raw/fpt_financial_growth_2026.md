# FPT Software Business Growth & Market Recognition (2025–2026)

FPT Software has achieved strong financial results in late 2025 and early 2026, driven by its strategic focus on AI-first digital transformations:

*   **AI and Data Analytics Sales Growth:** For the first four months of 2026, FPT Software's AI and data analytics division experienced an **83.3% sales growth** year-over-year, outperforming all other service lines.
