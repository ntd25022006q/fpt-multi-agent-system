# FPT Software AI Initiatives & Infrastructure (2025–2026)

## 1. FPT CASAN Maturity Framework (2026)
To guide enterprises through systematic GenAI and AI-agent adoption, FPT Software introduced the **CASAN Maturity Framework** in 2026. It structures AI readiness into 5 distinct levels:
1.  **Level 1: Curious (C)** — Initial exploration, testing basic conversational AI chatbots.
2.  **Level 2: Augmented (A)** — AI tools integration in software engineering (e.g. CodeVista pilot).
3.  **Level 3: Standard (S)** — Standardized AI tooling and basic multi-agent pipelines embedded in core workflows.
4.  **Level 4: Automatic (A)** — Autonomous workflow execution (e.g., ADLC service pods operating with dynamic routing).
5.  **Level 5: Native (N)** — Full cognitive architecture integration where AI drives system design and continuous learning.

*   *Source: FPT Software Official Enterprise Transformation Review (2026)*

## 2. FPT AI Factory
FPT Corporation established **AI Factories** in Vietnam and Japan equipped with high-performance NVIDIA GPU clusters (H100 & H200):
*   **Purpose:** Fine-tuning large language models, processing massive telemetry, and delivering sovereign cloud hosting.
*   **Scale:** Has processed over 1,100 billion tokens of multilingual datasets.
*   **Partnership:** Deeply integrated with NVIDIA's infrastructure layer to provide AI hosting for APAC clients.

*   *Source: FPT-NVIDIA Infrastructure Partnership Brief (Late 2025)*

## 4. FPT AI Residency: Lightweight Multimodal Learning
An international research project spearheaded by the FPT AI Residency focusing on wearable edge sensors and robotics:
*   **Key Innovation:** Training lightweight generative AI models that run locally on CPU/wearables without network latency.
*   **Academic Target:** Direct publication pipeline targeting CVPR, NeurIPS, ICCV/ECCV, IROS, and ICRA.
