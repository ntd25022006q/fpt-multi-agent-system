## 3. Strategic Consulting Approach
FPT Software follows a structured consulting methodology for enterprise clients:
*   **Phase 1 — Assessment (2–4 weeks):** Current-state architecture audit, technology debt analysis, risk identification, business capability mapping.
*   **Phase 2 — Strategy Design (2–6 weeks):** Target-state architecture design, vendor evaluation, roadmap planning, TCO/ROI modeling.
*   **Phase 3 — Proof of Concept (4–8 weeks):** Rapid prototype of highest-risk or highest-value architecture components.
*   **Phase 4 — Implementation (3–18 months):** Phased rollout with CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, and DevSecOps practices.
*   **Phase 5 — Operations & Optimization:** Managed services handoff with SLA monitoring, AI Ops, and continuous improvement.
*   Source: FPT Software Consulting Methodology (2026)
