# FPT Software — Company Overview & Core Capabilities (2025–2026)

## 1. Company Profile
FPT Software is a leading global IT services provider headquartered in Hanoi, Vietnam, and a wholly-owned subsidiary of FPT Corporation — one of Southeast Asia's largest technology conglomerates.
*   **Founded:** 1999 (as a software division of FPT); formally established as FPT Software in 2000.
*   **Employees:** Over 30,000 technology professionals worldwide (2026).
*   **Global Offices:** Presence in 30+ countries across Asia, Europe, Americas, Middle East, and Africa.
*   **Annual Revenue Target:** FPT Corporation Technology Segment contributes 63% of total corporate revenue.
*   Source: FPT Software Corporate Profile & Annual Report (2026)

---

## 2. Core Service Lines
FPT Software offers five primary IT service lines:
*   **Digital Transformation (DX):** End-to-end enterprise digitization, covering cloud migration, legacy modernization, and process automation using AI/ML.
*   **AI-First Solutions:** Deploying autonomous agents, large language model integrations (IvyHub, IvyChat), and custom AI model fine-tuning for enterprises.
*   **Software Development & Testing:** Agile delivery, DevSecOps, offshore and nearshore development centers.
*   **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP):** SAP and Oracle ERP implementations, upgrades, and managed services.
*   **Managed IT Services & AMS:** FPT's Application Management Services target 60–90% first-line support automation and 99.5% SLA compliance via intelligent agents.
*   Source: FPT Software Service Portfolio (2026)
