# FleziPT — FPT Software AI-First Platform Architecture

## Overview
FleziPT is FPT Software's flagship AI-First platform, launched on August 13, 2025. The name reflects "Flexible" + "PT" (FPT). It delivers end-to-end, customized AI-powered transformation solutions.

## Three-Layer Architecture

### Layer 1: Flezi Foundation (AI-Driven SDLC)
The foundational layer powering internal transformation:
- Redefines processes through an AI-driven SDLC
- Delivers cutting-edge technology with AI-powered IDEs and translation engines
- Establishes a robust, integrated architecture
- Enforces a **Secure-First approach** and Responsible AI compliance
- Key tools: CodeVista, AgentVista, TestVista

### Layer 2: Flezi Workforce (AI Accelerators)
Driving transformation at scale:
- **Horizontal AI Agents**: AgentVista (orchestration), CodeVista (coding), TestVista (testing)
- **Vertical AI Accelerators**: I2 (Industrial AI), IvyChat (Enterprise AI), AIDP
- Powers human-AI collaboration at scale with 25,000 AI-augmented engineers

### Layer 3: Flezi Verse (Industry Solutions)
Tailored transformations by industry:
- Manufacturing, Healthcare, Finance, Automotive, Energy & Utilities, Logistics, Insurance
- Industry-specific AI solutions built on foundation and accelerator layers

## Key Capabilities
- **Flexible AI Adoption**: Modular capabilities from ready-to-use AI applications to custom models
- **AI Agents Embedded Across Entire SDLC**: Every phase (planning, coding, testing, deployment, operations)
- **Multi-Model Support**: Works with waterfall, agile, or hybrid development models
- **Concurrent Workflows**: Run multiple AI-assisted tasks in parallel
- **Enterprise-Grade Security & Compliance**: Built-in governance, transparency, performance measurement
