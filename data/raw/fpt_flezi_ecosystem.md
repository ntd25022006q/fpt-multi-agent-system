# FPT Software FleziPT Ecosystem (2025–2026)

## 1. Introduction to FleziPT
The **FleziPT Ecosystem** is FPT Software's flagship comprehensive AI enablement framework launched to drive the transition from traditional development to AI-first software engineering and business operations. It acts as an umbrella for methodologies, specialized workforces, and tailored vertical solutions:
*   **AI-First Paradigm:** Embeds generative AI and autonomous agents directly into software engineering, customer support, and system integration.
*   **Infrastructure Support:** Backed by the FPT AI Factory high-performance computing (HPC) nodes (utilizing NVIDIA H100 and H200 GPU clusters).

---

## 2. Core Pillars of FleziPT
The ecosystem is structured around three key pillars that target different operational layers of an enterprise:

### A. Flezi Foundation
*   **Focus Area:** Best practices, governance, safety frameworks, and model fine-tuning.
*   **Function:** Ensures all AI initiatives comply with global data privacy and cybersecurity standards. It houses risk scoring matrices, prompt engineering libraries, and evaluation pipelines to assess model security (FPT Secure-First standards).
*   **Source:** FPT AI Governance Board Whitepaper (2025)

### B. Flezi Workforce
*   **Focus Area:** Specialized talent models and collaboration pods.
*   **Workforce Categories:**
    *   **AI Champions:** Internal consultants who map enterprise business challenges to agentic AI architectures.
    *   **AI-Augmented Engineers:** Developers utilizing code generation engines (like CodeVista) to accelerate delivery.
    *   **Digital Workers / Service Pods (ADLC & AMS):** Autonomous software agent units working in tandem with humans. For example, ADLC (Autonomous Development Lifecycle) pods aim to increase engineering output by 30% within identical budgets, while AMS (Application Management Services) pods automate 60-90% of first-line client support.
*   **Source:** FPT Global Talent & Delivery Platform Release (2026)

### C. Flezi Verse
*   **Focus Area:** Vertical-specific AI solutions and strategic application portfolios.
*   **Key Portfolio Areas:**
    *   **Manufacturing & Materials:** AI algorithms for predictive maintenance and supply chain route optimization.
    *   **Financial Services:** Sovereign cloud-compliant agentic setups for automated invoice auditing, credit assessment, and contract parsing.
    *   **Automotive (AIDM):** Integrating AI-defined mobility solutions in collaboration with the B2B automotive marketplace SDVerse.
*   **Source:** FPT Enterprise Vertical Integration Guide (2026)
