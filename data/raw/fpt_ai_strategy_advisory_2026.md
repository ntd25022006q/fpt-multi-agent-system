## 2. Digital Transformation Advisory Services

### 2.1 Core Banking Modernization
Common consulting scenario for BFSI clients:
*   **Challenge:** Migrating 30-40 year old COBOL mainframe systems to modern, cloud-native microservices architectures without disrupting 24/7 banking operations.
*   **FPT Approach:**
  *   Strangler Fig Pattern: Incrementally replace mainframe modules while the legacy system remains operational.
  *   Dual-Write Synchronization: New microservices write to both legacy and new databases during transition.
  *   API Facade Layer: Expose legacy functions through modern REST APIs during migration.
  *   Zero-Downtime Cutover: Blue-green deployment for final cutover with instant rollback capability.
*   **Typical Duration:** 18–36 months for full migration at a large bank.
*   **Risk Level:** High — requires strong governance, phased approach, and comprehensive testing.

### 2.2 Legacy System Modernization (Non-Banking)
*   **Common Patterns:** Lift-and-shift (rehost), re-platform (rebuild on cloud-native), or full re-architecture.
*   **FPT Recommendation:** Avoid "lift-and-shift" for systems with >5 years of accumulated technical debt; prefer re-platforming or re-architecture.

### 2.3 AI Integration into Existing Systems
*   **Approach:** FPT's "AI Augmentation" model: embed AI agents alongside existing processes rather than replacing entire workflows.
*   **Key Integration Points:** Document processing automation, intelligent search, predictive analytics dashboards, anomaly detection in monitoring systems.

## 3. Recommended KPIs for Digital Transformation Programs
FPT Software recommends measuring transformation success through the following metrics:
*   **Technical KPIs:**
  *   System availability (uptime): Target 99.9% (Tier 1) or 99.5% (Tier 2).
  *   Deployment frequency: Move from quarterly to weekly or daily releases.
  *   Mean time to recovery (MTTR): Reduce from hours to minutes.
  *   Test automation coverage: Achieve 80% automated test coverage.
*   **Business KPIs:**
  *   Cost per transaction: Reduce by 20–40% through automation.
  *   Customer satisfaction (NPS): Improve by 15–25 points through better digital experience.
  *   Time-to-market for new features: Reduce by 50–60% through DevOps adoption.
  *   Operational FTE reduction: 20–30% through RPA and AI automation.
*   **AI-Specific KPIs:**
  *   AI model accuracy (F1 score, precision, recall) measured against ground truth test sets.
  *   Hallucination rate for generative AI systems (target: < 3% in enterprise RAG systems).
  *   AI system latency: p99 response time < 3 seconds for interactive AI assistants.
